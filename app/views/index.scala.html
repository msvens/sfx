Welcome

You have reached SouthOfX. First of all, lets just get the name cleared up. The name stands for South of Södermalm. Södermalm being one of the major districts in central Stockholm SouthOfX marks the suburbs south of Södermalm.

So is this a site about the southern parts of Stockholm? No, which is also obvious from its content. SouthOfX simply marks where the people who created the site happened to live at the time (and still do). Although, it should in all honesty be admitted that in the back of our minds we had the intention to create something interesting for people living SouthOfX.

With the name sorted out lets get back to the site.As you might have guessed SouthOfX contains portfolios.
